Swajan is a private, independent, non-profit foundation based in Hooghly, West Bengal, India. The foundation provides help to students with financial need who show promise to work hard and stay focused to reach their full potential through education. Swajan’s scholarship and coaching services support the education of roughly 25 students each year. Our scholarships provide financial assistance and academic support to high school, undergraduate, and graduate students. Since opening the doors in 2002, it has provided support to many high-achieving and low-income students.

Our Mission: Enrich students’ mind to dream and achieve.
